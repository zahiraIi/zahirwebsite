# Detailed Guide: Filling OAuth Consent Screen App Information

When you click "CREATE" on the OAuth consent screen, you'll see a form. Here's exactly what to fill in:

## Step-by-Step Form Fields

### 1. App Information Section

**App name** ⭐ (Required)
```
Zahir Ali Portfolio
```
- This is the name users will see when authorizing your app
- Keep it simple and descriptive

**User support email** ⭐ (Required)
```
z5ali@ucsd.edu
```
- Click the dropdown arrow
- Select your email from the list, OR
- Type: `z5ali@ucsd.edu`
- This email will be shown to users if they need help

**App logo** (Optional)
- Click "UPLOAD" if you want to add a logo
- For now, you can skip this
- Recommended size: 120x120 pixels

### 2. App Domain Section

**Application home page** (Optional but recommended)
```
https://zahirali.com
```
- Your website's main URL
- Helps users identify your app

**Application privacy policy link** (Optional)
- You can skip this for personal use
- If you have one, add: `https://zahirali.com/privacy`

**Application terms of service link** (Optional)
- You can skip this for personal use
- If you have one, add: `https://zahirali.com/terms`

**Authorized domains** (Optional)
- You can skip this for personal use
- Only needed if you want to restrict which domains can use your app

### 3. Developer Contact Information Section

**Email addresses** ⭐ (Required)
```
z5ali@ucsd.edu
```
- Type your email address
- Google will send important notifications here
- Must be a Google account email

## Important Notes

1. **Required fields** are marked with a red asterisk (*) or the word "Required"
2. **Optional fields** can be left blank for personal use
3. **Email fields** must use a valid Google account email
4. You can always edit these later if needed

## After Filling the Form

1. Review all your entries
2. Click the blue **"SAVE AND CONTINUE"** button at the bottom
3. You'll proceed to the "Scopes" page next

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't** use a fake email address
❌ **Don't** leave required fields empty
❌ **Don't** use special characters in app name that might cause issues
✅ **Do** use your real email address
✅ **Do** use a simple, clear app name
✅ **Do** double-check your email addresses before continuing

## Need Help?

If you see any errors:
- Make sure all required fields (marked with *) are filled
- Check that email addresses are valid
- Try refreshing the page if something seems stuck
